Meet Jan Kokobane, Our Founder & CEO

Jan Kokobane

Founder & CEO, Investor Africa

Limpopo, South Africa

Jan, a visionary tech entrepreneur from Limpopo, South Africa, saw beyond the horizon of Africa’s startup scene. Where others saw challenges, he recognized an opportunity—a critical gap that, if bridged, could ignite a transformation across the continent.

"I set out to close the funding gap between African entrepreneurs and global investors. My mission is to create a thriving ecosystem where innovative African ideas don’t just survive—they flourish."

Driven by a deep passion for technology and entrepreneurship, Jan launched Investor Africa—a platform designed to uplift and empower the continent’s boldest startups, unlocking the potential for unparalleled economic growth and innovation.

  • Technology Expert — Forging the future through cutting-edge solutions.
  • Entrepreneurship Specialist — Nurturing startups to turn ideas into impactful businesses.
  • Startup Ecosystem Builder — Creating a collaborative space where innovators and investors unite to reshape Africa’s future.
